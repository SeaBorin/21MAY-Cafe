The Story Behind 21MAY.

21MAY. is a homegrown sanctuary born from a deep local passion for the art of exceptional morning rituals. Founded as a proud Cambodian creation, our shop is a space where the absolute highest standards of international coffee and tea culture are brought to life right here in the heart of Phnom Penh.

We believe your daily caffeine break should be an elevated experience, not just a routine. Driven by a dedication to the local specialty beverage movement, we meticulously source premier ceremonial Matcha and custom-roast every batch of coffee beans to pull out vibrant, complex notes. Whether you are here for an authentic whisked green tea or a masterfully pulled espresso, we welcome you to our table.
